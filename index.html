Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Can you work with an existing project?

Yes. Adding features, fixing bugs, optimizing performance, and refactoring messy code - I can help with all of that. Send me what you've got!

How does pricing work?

I price based on what you actually need, not a one-size-fits-all rate. I do both hourly and fixed-price depending on the project. Reach out with your idea, and I'll give you a free quote.

Do you build full games or just systems?

I specialize in systems and mechanics - the things that make games functional. Full game creation usually involves a broader team, but if you need the core scripting and functionality built out, that's exactly where I come in.

What's included for revisions?

Reasonable revisions are part of the deal. I'm not going to hand something off and disappear. If the scope changes significantly mid-project, we'll talk about it - but normal back-and-forth is included. I want you to be happy with what I deliver.

How do I reach you?

Discord is the fastest and my preferred method. Email works too.

How long will my project take?

Depends on the scope. Small, self-contained systems usually take 4-7 days. Mid-sized projects with multiple components are typically 1-2 weeks. Larger builds or full game systems can take me a longer time period. I'll give you a realistic timeline estimate before anything starts, so there are no surprises.

Do I get the source code?

Always. Everything I write for your project is yours - full ownership. You can modify it, build on it, do whatever you want with it.

What if I need something outside of Roblox?

I have experience across multiple platforms and languages. If your project involves web development, mobile apps, or other tools alongside Roblox, I can likely help. Check my portfolio for examples of off-platform work.

Terms of Service

Last updated: March 2026

01

Agreement

By hiring me for a project, you agree to the terms below. If any questions arise, please reach out before I begin development. Violating these terms can lead to a cancellation with no refund.

02

Payment

I accept PayPal (preferred), CashApp, and Robux (only if it is a substantial amount). Paypal/Cashapp is most convenient for me.

For projects under $50, full payment is required before I begin. For anything $50 or above, a 50% deposit is required before I begin, with the remaining balance due within 24 hours of delivery. Unpaid balances past that point will accrue a non-compounding 15% late fee per 24-hour window from the original due date. Ongoing non-payment means the project gets paused and may be reported to platform authorities.

No refunds on work that's been completed or is already in progress, unless the issue is on my end. All prices are in USD, and any taxes or fees on your side are your responsibility.

I don't take rev-share arrangements unless it is offered alongside an upfront payment.

03

Revisions & Bugs

Small adjustments within the agreed scope are included. If the project shifts significantly, I may quote that separately - it's why I make sure we're aligned before starting.

Any bugs caught at delivery get patched at no extra cost within 48 hours. After that window, fixes will be quoted and billed as additional work.

04

Cancellation

Cancelling after work has started means your deposit is non-refundable, and the cancellation may be flagged to relevant community platforms.

If I'm the one who cancels, you get a full refund - no questions asked.

05

Confidentiality & Portfolio

Finished work may be showcased in my portfolio, on this site, or across social media. Source code, personal information, and sensitive details always stay private. Please let me know beforehand if you'd like to keep any specifics private.

Everything you share with me during the project stays between us. If you need an NDA or confidentiality agreement, I'm happy to sign one.
